Here's a time line of Scott Hall's life:

  • 1958 Scott Hall was born in St. Mary's County, Maryland on October 20, 1958
  • 1984 Made his debut in NWA territory Championship Wrestling from Florida and soon feuded with Dusty Rhodes.
  • 1985 Joined the AWA where he wrestled as "Magnum" Scott Hall and later on, "Big" Scott Hall as a babyface.
  • 1986 On January 18, Won the AWA World Tag Team Championship teaming with Curt Hennig defeating Jimmy Garvin and Steven Regal.
  • 1989 He was brought in WCW by Jim Ross, wrestled house shows during early summer before debuting on July 9.
  • 1990 Wrestled in international promotions such as New Japan Pro Wrestling, Catch Wrestling Association, and World Wrestling Council.
  • 1991 Returned to WCW and was renamed The Diamond Studd
  • 1992 Joined WWF as Razor Ramon, a Cuban American bully from Miami which took mannerisms from his previous gimmick in WCW, Tony Mantana and Manny Ribera from the movie, Scarface.
  • 1993 Won his 1st Intercontinential Championship after being vacated by defeating Rick Martel.
  • 1994 At WrestleMania X, he defeated Shawn Michaels at WrestleMania to become the Undisputed Intercontinential Champion in a critically acclaimed ladder match.
  • 1995 By 1995, Hall was the first to set a record of being a 3 time Intercontinential Champion.
  • 1996 In early spring of 1996, Hall along with Diesel aka Kevin Nash signed with WCW and at a MSG show was involved in what is called "Curtain Call" after Hall, Diesel, HHH, and Michaels broke kayfabe and embraced. Shortly after, Hall, Nash and Hogan formed the NWO.
  • 2000 In early 2000, Hall left WCW and left as a former WCW US Champion, WCW TV Champion, and 7-time WCW World Tag Team Champion.
  • 2002 Returned to WWE alonside Hogan and Nash on February 17 at No Way Out PPV reunited as NWO but only he would only be there for only 3 months before being let go for substance abuse.
  • 2014 Hall returned to the WWE and Hall's old gimmick, Razor Ramon was inducted in the 2014 and made some appearances at different WrestleMania and Raw shows.
  • 2022 In March 2022 after having three heart attacks, he was put on life support. Then, he was taking off life support on March 14 and passed away at the age of 63.
